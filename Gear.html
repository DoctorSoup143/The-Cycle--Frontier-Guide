Armor

Armor

When a round impacts a player, the equipped armor reduces incoming damage. Incoming damage also reduces the currently equipped armor's durability.

Durability

If you take damage your armor loses durability. Loss of durability does not effect an armors protective qualities- unless the armor falls to 0 durability, in which case it will no longer provide protection.

Armor can be repaired at the crafting station.

Bonuses

Some protective armor pieces provide bonuses specific to the armor's tier. The known bonuses are:

- Stamina +25%
- HP +0.2/s
- Stamina +35%
- Stamina +40%
- Night Vision

Tiers

There are 4 armor tiers in The Cycle: Frontier, increasing in protection and durability as the item tier increases.

Backpacks

Common (Small) Backpack

Capacity: 200

Purchasing:
Backpacks provide 200 capacity. They can be purchased at any vendor.


Description:
Backpacks are equippable gear that increase your weight capacity. As player movement speed penalties and movement noise are related to how full an eqipped backpack is, higher capacity backpacks reduce the movement penalty and noise for the same amount of weight carried.

Uncommon (Medium) Backpack

Capacity: 250

Purchasing:
Uncommon backpacks provide 250 capacity. They must be crafted at the crafting station.


Description:
Uncommon backpacks provide 250 capacity. They can be crafted at the crafting station for 2,700 K-marks, 2 pieces of polymetallic prefabricate and 2 pieces of altered nickel Crafting an uncommon backpack takes 15mins.
